Antibiotic

Amoxicillin

medical_information

Key Indications

Bacterial infections (ENT, UTI, Skin), H. pylori eradication.

clinical_notes

Nursing Considerations

Assess for Penicillin allergy. Monitor for anaphylaxis and superinfection. Complete full course.

Category: Penicillins
Analgesic

Paracetamol

medical_information

Key Indications

Mild to moderate pain, fever reduction (Antipyretic).

clinical_notes

Nursing Considerations

Monitor liver function tests. Ensure max daily dose of 4g is not exceeded. Check OTC combinations.

Category: Non-opioid
Antihypertensive

Metoprolol

medical_information

Key Indications

Hypertension, Angina, Heart Failure prevention.

clinical_notes

Nursing Considerations

Monitor HR and BP before administration. Hold if HR < 60bpm. Watch for orthostatic hypotension.

Category: Beta-Blocker
